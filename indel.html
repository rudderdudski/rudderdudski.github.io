About Me

My name is Ashur Byrd, an 11-year-old entrepreneur specializing in maple syrup and cotton candy. My parents have been in the maple business for almost 16 years. I have combined their delicious products with my maple candy machine to create a heaven of flavorful maple cotton candy. I also offer different flavors of cotton candy that are popular in the community. Customer satisfaction is my top priority.
